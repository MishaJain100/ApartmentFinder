Frequently Asked Questions

To list your property, first create an account as a property owner. Once logged in, navigate to your dashboard and click on "Add Property." Fill out the property details form with accurate information and submit. Your listing will be active immediately.

You need to create a tenant account to schedule viewings. After logging in, browse properties and click on "Schedule a Viewing" on any property detail page. Select your preferred date and time, and the property owner will receive your request. You'll be notified once your appointment is confirmed.

Apartment Finder is free for tenants to browse listings and schedule viewings. Property owners can list their first property for free, with a small fee for additional listings. We also offer premium features for both tenants and owners that require a subscription.

Our neighbourhood data is collected from multiple reliable sources and is updated regularly. Ratings are based on a combination of official statistics, resident surveys, and expert evaluations. While we strive for accuracy, we recommend visiting the neighbourhood in person before making a final decision.

If you encounter a problem with a listing, you can report it by clicking the "Report" button on the property detail page. Alternatively, you can contact our support team through this contact form or by emailing support@apartmentfinder.com with details of the issue.
